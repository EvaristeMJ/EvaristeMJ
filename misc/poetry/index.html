Maxence Jauberty

It has been few years since I started writing poetry. I mostly write in French. I have published a few poems in the student journal of my school. My favorite poet is Jacques Prévert. I like to write under constraint, inspired by the Oulipo movement. Here is for example a poem I wrote influenced by "Mille milliards de poèmes" by Raymond Queneau: Miroir d'eau.
